    The judges

    Bill Draper

    Bill Draper began his venture capital career in 1962, when he started Draper & Johnson Investment Company. Bill was a founding investor in Hotmail, Skype, OpenTable, Athenahealth, Apollo Computer (acquired by Hewlett Packard), Integrated Genetics (Genzyme), Qume (I.T.T.), Activision (Mediagenic), Xidex (Eastman Kodak), Measurex, Hybritech (Eli Lilly), and more

    Tim Draper

    Tim Draper is Bill Draper's son and the founder of prominent venture capital firm DFJ. Tim Draper was a founding investor in Baidu, Tesla Motors, Skype, Parenting Magazine, Parametric Technology, Digidesign, Upside Publishing, PLX Technology, Four-1-1, and Hotmail.

    Jesse Draper

    Jesse Draper is founding partner of Halogen Ventures as well as creator and host of 2015 Emmy nominated television series, “The Valley Girl Show”. Jesse is a 4th generation venture capitalist focused on early stage investing in female founded consumer technology. Her portfolio includes Laurel & Wolf, Carbon38, Naya Health, HopSkipDrive, The Flex Company & Sugarfina.
